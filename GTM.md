# 🚀 GTM.md (Go-To-Market Strategy)

## 🎯 Target User

The exact target user is:

**Early-stage startup founders (0–20 employees) and engineering managers**
who:

* Actively use AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, etc.)
* Pay for multiple subscriptions without clear optimization
* Are cost-sensitive but lack visibility into their AI spend

Typical profile:

* Indie hackers building SaaS products
* YC-style founders
* Developers running side projects with paid AI tools

---

## 🔍 User Intent (When they need this tool)

The user is most likely to need this tool when they are:

* Reviewing monthly SaaS expenses
* Scaling their team and adding more AI seats
* Comparing tools like ChatGPT vs Claude vs Copilot
* Searching for ways to reduce startup burn

### Example search / scroll behavior:

* “reduce SaaS costs startup”
* “chatgpt vs claude pricing”
* “best AI tools for developers 2026”
* Browsing Hacker News discussions on AI tools

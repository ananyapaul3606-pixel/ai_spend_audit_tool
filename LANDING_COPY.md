## ❓ FAQ (5 Questions & Answers)

---

### 1. How does this tool work?

You enter the AI tools you use, your plan, and your monthly spend. The tool analyzes your setup and suggests ways to reduce costs based on pricing and usage patterns.

---

### 2. Do I need to create an account?

No. You can run a full audit without signing up. Email is only requested if you want to save or receive your report.

---

### 3. Is my data safe?

Yes. Personal details are never shared publicly. Any shared report link excludes identifying information.

---

### 4. How accurate are the recommendations?

Recommendations are based on current pricing data and common usage patterns. The logic is rule-based and designed to be transparent and explainable.

---

### 5. What happens after I see my results?

If significant savings are identified, you’ll have the option to explore optimization through Credex. Otherwise, you can simply use the insights to adjust your setup.

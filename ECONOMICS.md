# 💰 ECONOMICS.md

## 📌 Overview

This tool functions as a **lead generation engine** for Credex. It identifies companies overspending on AI tools and converts them into customers for discounted AI infrastructure credits.

The goal is not direct monetization, but **high-quality lead acquisition with strong conversion intent**.

---

## 💵 What is a Converted Lead Worth?

Assumption:

* Average startup AI spend: $500–$2,000/month
* Credex offers discounted credits (e.g., 20–30% savings)
* Credex earns margin or commission on credit resale (~10–20%)

### Example:

If a company spends $1,000/month:

* Annual spend = $12,000
* Credex margin (15%) = **$1,800/year per customer**

👉 **Estimated value per converted customer: $1,000–$2,000/year**

---

## 📊 Funnel Conversion Assumptions

From tool usage to revenue:

1. **Audit Completed → Email Capture**

   * Conversion: ~30%

2. **Email → Consultation Booked**

   * Conversion: ~20%

3. **Consultation → Paid Customer**

   * Conversion: ~30%

---

### 📉 Combined Conversion Rate:

0.30 × 0.20 × 0.30 = **1.8% overall conversion**

👉 Out of 100 users → ~2 paying customers

---

## 💸 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

### Channel: Organic (Reddit, Hacker News, X)

* Cost: $0 (time investment only)
* Effective CAC: **~$0–$10 per user**

---

### Channel: Direct Outreach

* Time cost only
* Higher conversion due to personalization

---

### Estimated CAC per paying customer:

If:

* 100 users → 2 customers
* Cost ≈ $50 effort equivalent

👉 CAC ≈ **$25 per customer**

---

## 📈 Revenue Projection (Early Stage)

### Scenario: 1,000 users/month

* Email captures (30%) → 300
* Consultations (20%) → 60
* Customers (30%) → 18

If each customer = $1,500/year:

👉 Monthly added ARR = 18 × 1500 = **$27,000 ARR**

---

## 🚀 Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

To reach $1M ARR:

### Required customers:

* $1,000,000 / $1,500 ≈ **667 customers**

---

### Required traffic:

If conversion = 1.8%:

* Needed users = 667 / 0.018 ≈ **37,000 users**

---

### Monthly target:

* ~2,000–3,000 users/month over 18 months

---

### What must be true:

* Strong organic growth (viral sharing)
* High-value audits (> $500 savings cases)
* Efficient lead capture + follow-up
* Credex conversion pipeline is optimized

---

## 📊 Profitability Analysis

### Per Customer:

* Revenue: ~$1,500/year
* CAC: ~$25

👉 **LTV:CAC ≈ 60:1 (very strong)**

---

### Key Insight:

Even if assumptions are optimistic:

* The margin is large enough to sustain experimentation
* Organic channels significantly reduce acquisition cost

---

## ⚠️ Risks & Assumptions

* Conversion rates may be lower in reality
* Users may not trust recommendations initially
* Pricing variations across regions
* Dependence on organic distribution

---

## 📈 Optimization Opportunities

* Improve audit accuracy → increase trust
* Better CTA for high-savings users
* Follow-up email sequences
* Retargeting high-intent leads

---

## 🧠 Final Insight

This product works economically because:

👉 It targets users already spending money
👉 It identifies inefficiency (high intent)
👉 It converts only the most valuable leads

This makes it a **high-leverage, low-CAC growth engine** for Credex.
